Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Introduction

At SnickerShow, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Travel License generator.

Information We Collect

What We DON'T Collect:

  • No Personal Information: We don't require account creation or login.
  • No Email Addresses: We never ask for your email.
  • No Phone Numbers: We don't collect phone numbers.
  • No Payment Information: Our service is completely free.
  • No Uploaded Images: Photos you upload are processed locally in your browser only.

What We May Collect:

  • Basic Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (page views, browser type, device type) to improve the service.
  • Local Storage: Your card settings are saved in your browser's local storage for convenience. This data never leaves your device.

How Your Data is Processed

Client-Side Processing:

All ID card customization, photo uploads, and downloads happen directly in your browser. Your photos and personal information never touch our servers.

Local Storage:

We use browser local storage to save your preferences (like chosen themes and entered information) so you don't have to re-enter them each visit. This data:

  • Stays on your device only
  • Never gets sent to our servers
  • Can be cleared anytime by clearing your browser data

Cookies

We may use minimal cookies for:

  • Analytics: To understand how people use the site (via services like Google Analytics)
  • Functionality: To remember your preferences

We do NOT use cookies for advertising or tracking across websites.

Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics
  • CDN Services: For faster loading of libraries (html2canvas, jsPDF)

These services may have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear Your Data: Clear browser local storage anytime
  • Use Without Tracking: Use browser privacy mode or ad blockers
  • Ask Questions: Contact us about privacy concerns

Data Security

Since we don't collect or store your personal data, there's minimal risk to your privacy. Your photos and information are processed entirely on your device.

We implement standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure hosting infrastructure
  • Regular security updates

Children's Privacy

Our service is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't require account creation, we have no way to verify user age, but we take privacy seriously for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Email: contact@snickershow.store
